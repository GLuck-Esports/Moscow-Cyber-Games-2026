### Moscow Cyber Games 2026 

---
## Tournament Operator

- **Tournament Operator:** GLuck Esports

## Event Structure and Format

- **Moscow Cyber Games 2026 European Qualifier:**

  - Online Open Qualifier: Bo1 Single Elimination with unlimited sign ups, 2 teams qualifies to Closed Qualifier.
  - Online Open Qualifier Dates: 12th, 13th, 14th, 15th August 2026

  - Online Closed Qualifier: 8 qualified teams from 4 Open Qualifiers + 8 VRS invited teams by VRS Global, Swiss system BO3 + Single Elimination BO3. 2 teams qualifies to LAN Main Event Moscow Cyber Games 2026
  - Online Closed Qualifier Dates: 22nd August - 1st September 2026

  - Additional Information: For complete VRS invitation details, seeding information, tournament format, and prize pool distribution, see the [Additional Information document](moscow-cyber-games-2026-european-qualifier-additional-information.md).

- **Moscow Cyber Games 2026 Asian Qualifier:**

  - Online Open Qualifier: Bo1 Single Elimination with unlimited sign ups, 2 teams qualifies to Closed Qualifier.
  - Online Open Qualifier Dates: 12th, 13th August 2026

  - Online Closed Qualifier: 4 qualified teams from 2 Open Qualifiers + 4 VRS invited teams by VRS Asia, 2 GSL Groups Bo3 + Single Elimination BO3. 2 teams qualifies to LAN Main Event Moscow Cyber Games 2026
  - Online Closed Qualifier Dates: 22nd August - 26th August 2026

  - Additional Information: For complete VRS invitation details, seeding information, tournament format, and prize pool distribution, see the [Additional Information document](moscow-cyber-games-2026-asian-qualifier-additional-information.md).

- **Moscow Cyber Games 2026:**
  - LAN Main Event format: 2 qualified teams from European Closed Qualifier + 2 qualified teams from Asian Closed Qualifier. Single Elimination BO3 + 3rd place decider
  - LAN Main Event dates: 31st October - 1st November 2026
  - Main Event Prize Pool: 15 000 000 ₽ ≈ $200 000 USD (Converted $ USD prize is based on the exchange rate on June 21st, 2026)

  - Additional Information: For complete VRS invitation details, seeding information, tournament format, and prize pool distribution, see the [Additional Information document](moscow-cyber-games-2026-additional-information.md).


## VRS Information
- **Tournament Tier for Moscow Cyber Games 2026:** Tier 2
- **Ranked Stages:** **HLTV.org**, after our formal application, will determine which stages and matches will count toward VRS. Ranking status is not guaranteed until confirmed 
- **VRS list used:** VRS Global, VRS Asia
- **Regional restrictions:** Players from Estonia, Latvia, Lithuania, or Ukraine are not eligible to participate in the tournament. Exceptions may be granted to players of these nationalities who hold permanent residency in another country or who have been living abroad for an extended period due to work or studies. Such players may be required to provide official documentation confirming their status upon request by the tournament organizer. This restriction is intended to address visa and logistical challenges, as well as to maintain fair and balanced playing conditions for all participants, particularly with regard to network latency (ping). The tournament organizer reserves the right to review each exception request individually and to approve or deny participation at their sole discretion.
