### Moscow Cyber Games 2026 – Additional Information

---
## Tournament Operator

- **Tournament Operator:** GLuck Esports

## Tournament Format

- **Moscow Cyber Games 2026:**
  - LAN Main Event format: 2 qualified teams from European Closed Qualifier + 2 qualified teams from Asian Closed Qualifier. Single Elimination BO3 + 3rd place decider
  - LAN Main Event dates: October, 31st - November, 1st 2026
  - Main Event Prize Pool: 150.000 USD

## VRS Information
- **Tournament Tier:** Tier 2
- **Ranked Stages:** **HLTV.org**, after our formal application, will determine which stages and matches will count toward VRS. Ranking status is not guaranteed until confirmed 
- **VRS list used:** VRS Global, VRS Asia
- **VRS publication date used for seeding:** October 2026 

## Visa / Location

- **Visa region:** Russian Federation
- **LAN location:** Moscow, Russian Federation

## Prize Pool and Compensation

**Total Prize Pool:** 15 000 000 ₽ ≈ $200 000 USD (Converted $ USD prize is based on the exchange rate on June 21st, 2026)
