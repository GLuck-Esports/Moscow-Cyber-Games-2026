### Moscow Cyber Games 2026 European Qualifier – Additional Information

## Tournament Operator

- **Tournament Operator:** GLuck Esports

## Tournament Format

- **Moscow Cyber Games 2026 European Qualifier:**

  - Online Open Qualifier: Bo1 Single Elimination with unlimited sign ups, 2 teams qualifies to Closed Qualifier.
  - Online Open Qualifier Dates: August, 12th, 13th, 14th, 15th 2026
  - Online Closed Qualifier: 8 qualified teams from 4 Open Qualifiers + 8 VRS invited teams by VRS Global, Swiss system BO3 + Single Elimination BO3. 2 teams qualifies to LAN Main Event Moscow Cyber Games 2026
  - Online Closed Qualifier Dates: August, 22nd - September, 1st 2026


## VRS Information
- **Tournament Tier** Tier 2
- **Ranked Stages:** **HLTV.org**, after our formal application, will determine which stages and matches will count toward VRS. Ranking status is not guaranteed until confirmed 
- **VRS list used:** VRS Global
- **VRS publication date used for ranking:** July 2026  
- **VRS publication date used for seeding:** August 2026
