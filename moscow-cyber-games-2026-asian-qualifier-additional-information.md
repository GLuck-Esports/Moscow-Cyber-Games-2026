### Moscow Cyber Games 2026 Asian Qualifier – Additional Information

---
## Tournament Operator

- **Tournament Operator:** GLuck Esports

## Tournament Format

- **Moscow Cyber Games 2026 Asian Qualifier:**

  - Online Open Qualifier: Bo1 Single Elimination with unlimited sign ups, 2 teams qualifies to Closed Qualifier.
  - Online Open Qualifier Dates: August, 12th, 13th 2026
  - Online Closed Qualifier: 4 qualified teams from 2 Open Qualifiers + 4 VRS invited teams by VRS Asia, 2 GSL Groups Bo3 + Single Elimination BO3. 2 teams qualifies to LAN Main Event Moscow Cyber Games 2026
  - Online Closed Qualifier Dates: August, 22nd - August, 26th 2026


## VRS Information
- **Tournament Tier** Tier 2
- **Ranked Stages:** **HLTV.org**, after our formal application, will determine which stages and matches will count toward VRS. Ranking status is not guaranteed until confirmed 
- **VRS list used:** VRS Asia
- **VRS publication date used for ranking:** July 2026  
- **VRS publication date used for seeding:** August 2026
